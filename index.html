SIP Abacus Mental Arithmetic Contest 2019
2 students awarded in SIP Abacus Mental Arithmetic Contest 2019

Gives me immense pleasure to inform that two students namely Dikshita Singh and Bibhu Sagar of our school added feathers to the cap of the school by notching the First and the Third positions in respectively in 19th International SIP Abacus Mental Arithmetic Contest held in Chennai Trade Centre , Chennai. Around 37000 students from UAE, Tanzania, Srilanka, Singapore, Philippines, Baharain, Malasia and Hinging participated in this prestigious competition in which our students performed so well that they clinched the positions by defeating other participants. This competition is purely based on ability of the students' arithmetic calculation. Congratulations to both of the students for such a great achievement.









VVM 2020 Jharkhand Qualification
13 students qualified in VVM 2020 Jharkhand

With good wishes and blessings 13 students have qualified the first round of Vidhyarthi Vigyan Manthan 2020(Indiaâ€™s Largest Science Talent Search Examination for New India Using Digital Devices) which is the highest in almost all DAV schools of Jharkhand. These students will participate in the State Level VVM Camp. Our students have always been performing marvelously in VVM for last 4-5 years. Vidyarthi Vigyan Manthan (VVM) is an initiative of Vijnana Bharati (VIBHA), in collaboration with Vigyan Prasar, an autonomous organization under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India and National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), an institution under the Ministry of Education (earlier known as Ministry of Human Resources and Development). VVM is a national program for popularizing science among school students of standard VI to XI, conceptualized to identify the bright minds with a scientific aptitude among the student community. All these 13 students should be appreciated for their hard labour who even in this time of COVID has paved their way to the next Level of VVM. The credit should also be given to the teachers who have guided the students in proper way due to which they were successful in qualifying VVM.
